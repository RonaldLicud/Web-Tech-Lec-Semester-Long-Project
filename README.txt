After unzipping the file.
Click the index.html file preferably open it with google chrome.
Then you can see the homepage. And the navigation on the upper rightside.
On the navigation you can see the categories and the team.
When you click on the categories you can see the topics, click the diffent boxes to see the contents of each topic.
And when you click on the team you can see the members of the group.
The template of the site is from https://startbootstrap.com/
